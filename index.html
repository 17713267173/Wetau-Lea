About UsWho We Are

The China Human Resources Youth Community is a professional, international and socialized think tank based on China and looking at the world. It was co-sponsored by the International Education Exchange Committee of the Global Talent Association, HR Pedia, China United Business School, and Harvard University post-doctoral fellows and members of the democratic parties. At the same time, under the strong support and guidance of international organizations such as the “G20 YEA Talent Project Consultant Team, Beijing Returnees Association, Chinese Academy of Management Sciences, Viva La Vida, etc., the community actively implements and develops the philosophy of Co-creation, mutual learning, ecology and practice and it is committed to providing professional and international support and services for the development of China's human resources industry, corporate transformation and innovation, talent training and development.
